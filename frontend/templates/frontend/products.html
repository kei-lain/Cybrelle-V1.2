cybrelle

Cybrelle is a Vulnerability triage and management solution for small businesses. It helps reduce network's attack surface, identify critical vulnerabilities, and make informed decisions on how to act on them. It easily allows the users the option to reduce the amount of vulnerabilities that your system is susceptible to.
